  • Grind: Coarse

  • Time: 4 minutes

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you source your coffee beans?

We directly partner with sustainable farms across premier coffee-growing regions, ensuring fair trade practices and highest quality beans.

What's the best way to store coffee beans?

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Avoid refrigeration as beans can absorb moisture and odors.

How long does your coffee stay fresh?

Our coffee is best enjoyed within 30 days of the roast date. Each package is sealed to maintain freshness and labeled with roasting date.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Delivery times vary by location, and we ensure proper packaging to maintain freshness during transit.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund or replacement.

Which brewing method do you recommend for beginners?

For beginners, we recommend starting with a French Press. It's simple to use, forgiving with grind size, and helps you understand the basics of coffee brewing. Once comfortable, you can explore pour-over or other methods.
